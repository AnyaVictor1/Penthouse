Core Value

Since inception, The Penthouse has been guided by its core values. These are values we hold which form the bedrock
upon which we deliver bespoke professional services. They are not descriptions of the work we do or the strategies we employ to accomplish our mission.
They underlie our work, how we interact with each other, and which strategies we employ to achieve our mission. They are the basic elements of how we go about our work. These core values are:

GREATNESS

We believe in a life beyond self; a step by step way of living one’s life not just for our own good but the good of others.

CREATIVITY

We have taken the path of thinking with, outside or without the box in delivering results to our clients. We are committed to going the extra mile to generate or recognize ideas, alternatives, or possibilities that may be useful in solving problems.

OWNERSHIP

At The Penthouse there are neither employees nor employers; we are all players and stakeholders in our own way—we are partners on a mission—we all have a critical stake in the outcome. So, our clients are indeed ‘OUR’ clients and our people are at home at work.

POSSIBILITIES

We are committed to optimism at the highest level; we do not give up except it’s the only option left. To us every possibility is realizable.
